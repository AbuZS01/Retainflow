How it works

01
Add an ayah range

Search by surah name, pick a curated starter pack, or set a custom range. The Arabic text loads instantly for reference.

02
Review when it's due

Open the app. Recite from memory. Mark how it went — Forgot, Hard, Good, or Easy. That's the whole session.

03

Strong recall pushes the next review further out. Weak recall brings it back sooner. Your effort goes exactly where it's needed.
